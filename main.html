Immerse yourself

Snow Water of Switzerland, is sourced exclusively for you from the Swiss Alps in beautiful St. Moritz. The snow water is created by the seasonal snowfall between December and March of every year and will be gathered and melted on-site upon request.
The melted snow is then filtered and tested
to ensure the purest quality of each drop
before embarking on its journey to your swimming sanctuary.

Certificate of Origin

An official stamp and signature
from the St. Moritz municipality
certify the authenticity of Snow Water's origin. The certificate is paired with a finely crafted mountain relief of St. Moritz, Switzerland, representing Snow Water's source.

The certificate of origin and the mountain relief are presented in a beautiful encasement.
This encasement is customisable and provides an opportunity for you to create a perfect and unique gift.

Service Pledge

We are dedicated to provide Snow Water of only the finest quality. This commitment extends to our thorough filtration process, and scientifically-based four-point testing, guaranteeing swimming pool water of drinkable quality.

We have partnered
with an exceptional transportation company
to ensure a seamless journey from the mountains of St. Moritz to your private oasis. Whether you choose your preferred delivery route by air, land or ship - we’ve got it covered.

Your Peace of Mind

Through the electrically powered vehicles that make up the transport of Snow Water’s journey within Switzerland, our eco-friendly approach ensures that nature remains unharmed by carbon impact.
Approved by the St. Moritz community, and boasting minimal emissions during the entire inland transportation phase, we are
proud to say that the melting process
of the snow, in particular, is achieved with zero carbon emission and zero noise pollution.

Welcome to a world where luxury meets environmental responsibility.
